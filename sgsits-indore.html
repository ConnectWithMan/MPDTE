SGSITS Indore

Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science

Admissions • Cutoff • Placements • Fees

Overview of SGSITS Indore

Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science (SGSITS), located in Indore, is arguably the most prestigious and sought-after engineering college in Madhya Pradesh. Established in 1952, it is a government-aided autonomous institute affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV).

Known for its rigorous academic curriculum, esteemed faculty, and outstanding placement records, SGSITS attracts the top rankers of the state. It offers various undergraduate (B.Tech, B.Pharm) and postgraduate programs.

How to Get Admission in SGSITS

Securing a seat in SGSITS requires excellent academic performance and strategic participation in counselling.

  • Entrance Exam: Admissions to the B.Tech programs are granted strictly on the basis of the All India Rank (AIR) secured in JEE Main.
  • Counselling Process: Candidates must participate in the centralised MP DTE Counselling conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh.
  • Quotas: Approx. 90% of seats are reserved for MP Domicile candidates under the State Quota, while the remaining 10% are open for All India Quota (AIQ) students.
  • B.Pharm Admission: Admission to B.Pharm is typically based on the qualifying exam (Class 12th) merit or state-level exams as specified by DTE.

SGSITS Indore Cutoff Trends

Because of its premier status, the cutoff for SGSITS is usually the highest among all MP state colleges.

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE): Remains the top choice. General category candidates (MP Domicile) typically need a JEE Main rank under 30,000 in the initial rounds.
  • Information Technology (IT): Closely follows CSE, usually closing around 40,000 to 45,000 for UR candidates.
  • Core Branches (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical): These offer a bit of relief with cutoffs extending between 60,000 and 1,20,000 depending on the specific branch and category.
  • Note: Cutoffs for AIQ (outside state) candidates are significantly higher (meaning a better rank is needed) as they are considered under the Unreserved category.

Placements and Fee Structure

SGSITS boasts some of the best return on investment (ROI) metrics in the region.

  • Placement Highlights: The institute attracts top-tier product-based and service-based companies like Goldman Sachs, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, and Deloitte.
  • Package Stats: The highest package often touches 40-50 LPA, while the average package for CSE/IT branches easily crosses 10-12 LPA. The overall college average stands around 8 LPA.
  • Fee Structure: Being a government-aided institute, the tuition fees are quite reasonable. The B.Tech course fee is approximately ₹1,00,000 to ₹1,15,000 per annum, making it highly affordable compared to private universities.
  • Scholarships: Eligible students can avail of various state scholarships like MMVY (Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana) and Post Matric Scholarships.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How to get admission in SGSITS Indore?

Admission to B.Tech courses at SGSITS Indore is done through the MP DTE Counselling process based on the JEE Main AIR.

2. Is SGSITS Indore a government or private college?

SGSITS is a government-aided autonomous institute, affiliated with RGPV, Bhopal.

3. What is the average placement package at SGSITS?

The average placement package at SGSITS Indore is around 8 to 10 LPA, while the highest packages can go up to 40-50 LPA.

4. Does SGSITS have a reservation for MP Domicile students?

Yes, around 90% of the seats are reserved for students holding a valid Madhya Pradesh Domicile under the state quota.

5. What are the most popular branches at SGSITS?

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Information Technology (IT) are the most popular and competitive branches at SGSITS.
